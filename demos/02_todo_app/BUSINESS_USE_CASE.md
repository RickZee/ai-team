# Business Use Case: Full-Stack TODO Application

A small product squad wants to ship a **task-tracking tool for internal teams** — create, view, complete todos via a browser, backed by a database. Think of it as a typical greenfield MVP that a startup or innovation lab would commission to test whether agents can replace a short contractor engagement.

## Business need

Tasks live in spreadsheets, chat threads, and ad-hoc notes. Pulling a lightweight product together has historically taken days to weeks of coordinated backend, frontend, and data work. We want to compress that with agents and have a real vertical slice to point at.

## What matters

- Users can add, view, and complete tasks from a browser
- Data survives restarts
- A developer can spin up the whole stack with one command

## Who asked for this

A product owner validating MVP scope before funding a squad. Also serves as the target application for demo 06 (performance optimization loop).
